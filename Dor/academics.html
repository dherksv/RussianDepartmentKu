    INTEGRATED DIPLOMA IN RUSSIAN

    This is an intensive course at the Post-graduate level and is known as `lntegrated Diploma Course in Russian". The course gives opportunity to students to have а better understanding of the language. It is intended to develop the communication skills of the students in relation to both written and spoken Russian at а higher level than in the existing Certificate and Diploma courses. Emphasis is given to the training in translation of Russian material in Science and Technology. The course will also help the students to acquaint themselves with а few short stories and poems of the Russian authors. The duration of the course is one academic year with about 450 working hours i.e. 100 hours more than the working hour taken together for the Certificate and Diploma courses. Seats : 45 Click here for Syllabus ADVANCED DIPLOMA IN RUSSIAN TRANSLATION (Course is yet to be started) This а terminal course in Russian and will be known as "Advanced Diploma in Russian Translation". The duration of course is one academic year. The minimum qualification for admission to the Course will be Diploma in Russian or an equivalent qualification. The selection will be made on the basis of the marks scored in the qualifying examination. The course is designed to provide specialized training for persons intending to translate literary and technical literature in Russian to English/ Malayalam and vice-versa. It is also meant for those who wish to acquire а competence in oral interpretation. The course also involves an acquaintance with the theory of translation, the art of translation, the technique of translation, the problems of translation etc. The emphasis will be wholly on the practical aspects of translation.
