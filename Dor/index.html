    Teaching of Russian Language was started in the University of Kerala in 1968. Popularity of Russian has had great influence on the students of Kerala University. Russia is one of the greatest scientifically advanced countries of the world and our country has been highly benefited both scientifically and economically. There are many treaties between the Government of India and Russia for our mutual benefit. There is a great demand for Russian language translators, scientific interpreters, teachers to teach Russian in India and abroad. A candidate who has a pass in the B.A./B.Sc. Language Course II (Russian) can join for M.A. Russian of Kerala University as University Department of Russian is conducting courses like M.A. (CSS), M.A. Part-time, M.Phil., Ph.D. and Post-Doctoral Research.
